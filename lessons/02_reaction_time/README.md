# Lesson 02: Reaction Time Lab

**Estimated time:** 90 minutes
**Game:** ReactionTimeGame — Standard preset (60 trials), launched from the main menu, press ENTER on lesson 02
**Prerequisites:** Lesson 01 (Big Data in Cognitive Science)

## Learning Goals

- Explain what reaction time measures and why it is a window into cognition.
- Describe Donders' subtraction method and its historical significance.
- Interpret a reaction-time histogram: identify mean, skewness, and outliers.
